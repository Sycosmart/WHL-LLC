Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free consultation work?

We visit your home, measure your windows, and walk through your options in person, so there's no guesswork about fit or style. It's free and there's no obligation to buy.

How long does installation take?

Most homes are completed in a single visit. Exact timing depends on how many windows are involved; we'll give you an estimate during your consultation.

Are cordless blinds better for homes with kids or pets?

Yes. Cordless lift systems remove dangling cords entirely, which most families and pet owners prefer over traditional corded blinds.

What if I don't see what I want in your catalog?

The catalog above shows our most popular styles, but we can source and fit other options too. Book a free consultation and let's talk about what you have in mind.

What areas do you serve?

We serve homes across Maryland. Reach out and we can confirm whether we cover your specific area.
